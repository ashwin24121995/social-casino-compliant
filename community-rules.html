Community Rules

Guidelines for a Positive Gaming Experience

Welcome to Our Community! We're committed to maintaining a respectful, safe, and enjoyable environment for all players. Please read and follow these community rules.

1. Respectful Conduct

All users must treat other players and staff with respect. Harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior is strictly prohibited. This includes:

  • Personal attacks or insults
  • Hate speech or discriminatory language
  • Threats or intimidation
  • Sexual harassment or inappropriate behavior

2. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Cheating or exploiting game mechanics
  • Using bots or automated tools
  • Attempting to circumvent security measures
  • Sharing account credentials with others
  • Impersonating other users or staff members
  • Spamming or excessive messaging
  • Posting offensive, inappropriate, or illegal content
  • Attempting to access unauthorized areas of the platform
  • Distributing malware or harmful software

3. Account Security

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials. We recommend:

  • Creating a strong, unique password
  • Never sharing your password with anyone
  • Logging out after each session
  • Reporting unauthorized access immediately
  • Enabling two-factor authentication if available

4. Fair Play Commitment

All games use certified random number generators to ensure fair outcomes. Any attempt to manipulate game results or exploit vulnerabilities will result in account suspension or termination.

5. Consequences for Violations

Violations of these community rules may result in:

  • Warning: First minor violation
  • Temporary Suspension: Repeated violations or serious misconduct (1-7 days)
  • Extended Suspension: Severe violations (7-30 days)
  • Permanent Termination: Extreme violations or repeated serious offenses
  • Forfeiture of Virtual Coins: In cases of cheating or fraud

6. Reporting Violations

If you witness a violation of these community rules, please report it immediately to our support team:

Email: support@example.com
Include: Username of violator, description of violation, date/time, and any evidence

We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

7. Content Guidelines

When posting in community areas, ensure your content:

  • Is appropriate for all ages
  • Does not contain hate speech or discrimination
  • Does not promote illegal activities
  • Does not spam or advertise external services
  • Respects intellectual property rights
  • Does not contain personal information of others

8. Amendments to Rules

We reserve the right to modify these community rules at any time. Changes will be posted on our website with an updated date. Continued use of our platform constitutes acceptance of updated rules.

9. Appeals Process

If you believe your account was suspended or terminated unfairly, you may appeal:

  • Submit an appeal within 30 days of the action
  • Include your username and detailed explanation
  • Send to: support@example.com with subject "Account Appeal"
  • Our team will review and respond within 7 business days
